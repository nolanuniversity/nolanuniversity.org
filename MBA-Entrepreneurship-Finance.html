📚 The Nolan MBA · Programme Structure

A carefully scaffolded journey from financial foundations to venture launch. Each term builds the skills you need to build, fund, and scale.

🔨 TERM 1 · FINANCIAL TOOLKIT
⚙️ TERM 2 · DEEP DIVES
🚀 TERM 3 · LAUNCH & SCALE
TERM 1 30 CREDITS · 4 MODULES 🔨 THE FINANCIAL TOOLKIT

Build the financial and entrepreneurial foundations every founder and investor must master. You'll learn the language of capital and the art of venture creation.

Entrepreneurial Finance & Fundraising 8 credits
Master cap tables, term sheets, valuation, convertible notes, and fundraising mechanics. Build investor-ready financial models.
INVESTOR BUILDER
Entrepreneurial Mindset & Opportunity 8 credits
Opportunity recognition, customer discovery, effectuation, and creative problem-solving. Conduct 20+ customer interviews.
BUILDER INNOVATOR
Financial Accounting for Entrepreneurs 7 credits
Understand financial statements, accounting principles, and how to make data-driven financial decisions for early-stage ventures.
INVESTOR BUILDER
Corporate Finance Fundamentals 7 credits
Capital budgeting, cost of capital, valuation basics, and financing decisions tailored for high-growth companies.
INVESTOR
📊 Typical weekly workload: 10-12 hours (Entrepreneurial Finance) + 8-10 hours each for other modules = ~40 hours total. Balanced for deep learning.
TERM 2 30 CREDITS · 4 MODULES ⚙️ THE INVESTOR'S DEEP DIVES

Specialised knowledge to evaluate, fund, and scale ventures. You'll move from toolkit to deep expertise in VC, growth strategy, and deal structuring.

Venture Capital & Private Equity 8 credits
LBO modelling, fund economics, carry waterfalls, deal sourcing, due diligence, and LP-GP dynamics. Build a full fund model.
INVESTOR BUILDER
Growth Strategy & Scaling Ventures 8 credits
Product-market fit, growth metrics, scaling strategies, unit economics, and go-to-market execution for high-growth companies.
BUILDER INNOVATOR
Financial Modeling for Startups 7 credits
Build sophisticated three-statement models, scenario analysis, sensitivity tables, and investor-ready projections using advanced Excel.
INVESTOR BUILDER
Deal Structuring & Negotiation 7 credits
Term sheet anatomy, valuation methodologies, negotiation tactics, and deal structuring for founders and investors. Simulated deal negotiations.
INVESTOR BUILDER
📊 Typical weekly workload: 12-14 hours (VC/PE) + 8-10 hours each for other modules = ~40 hours. Deep dives require intense focus.
TERM 3 30 CREDITS · 3 MODULES 🚀 THE CAPSTONE & SCALE

Apply everything you've learned. Launch a real venture, raise capital, or complete an investment thesis. Present to investors at Demo Day.

Venture Practicum (Capstone) 15 credits
Launch a real venture, raise capital, or complete a comprehensive investment thesis. Full financial projections, unit economics, pitch deck, and Demo Day presentation.
BUILDER INVESTOR INNOVATOR
Advanced Valuation & Exit Strategies 10 credits
DCF, comparable analysis, LBO models, M&A strategies, IPOs, and exit planning. Learn to maximize value for founders and investors.
INVESTOR BUILDER
Global Immersion (Elective) 5 credits
One-week immersion in Silicon Valley, London, or Nairobi. Meet founders, VCs, and innovation leaders. Ecosystem mapping and networking.
INNOVATOR INVESTOR
📊 Typical weekly workload: Venture Practicum dominates (15-20 hours), plus 10-12 hours for Valuation module. Capstone intensity prepares you for real-world launch.
