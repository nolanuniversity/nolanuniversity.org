🚀 3-year BSc · Entrepreneurship & Venture Creation

📋 Overview · Build what matters

For those who don't just study business – they create it. The BSc in Entrepreneurship & Venture Creation is designed for aspiring founders, innovators, and startup leaders. You'll learn by doing: launching ventures, pitching to investors, and building real products.

You'll master lean startup methodology, entrepreneurial finance, growth marketing, and venture scaling – all while being mentored by seasoned entrepreneurs and investors. By graduation, you'll have launched a real venture or built a substantial portfolio of entrepreneurial projects.

Turn data into strategy. The BSc in Business Analytics & AI equips you with the technical and analytical skills to solve real business problems using data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

You'll master Python, SQL, predictive modelling, and AI applications – alongside business fundamentals like finance, marketing, and strategy. Graduates are sought after as data analysts, business intelligence specialists, and AI consultants across every industry.
