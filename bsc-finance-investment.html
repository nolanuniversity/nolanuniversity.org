🎓 3-year BSc · Finance & Investment

📋 Overview · Build your financial foundation

At Nolan University, we educate Company Builders, Investors, and Innovators. The BSc in Finance & Investment is your first step – mastering the language of capital, the tools of analysis, and the ethics of leadership.

This rigorous, CFA-affiliated programme blends quantitative training with real-world practice. From day one, you'll engage with Bloomberg terminals, trading simulations, and spring week preparation. You'll graduate ready to launch your career in investment banking, asset management, or corporate finance.

🔎 Detailed modules · 20+ courses

Every module combines theory with practical application. Here are core modules you'll study:

Principles of Finance

15 credits
Time value of money, NPV/IRR, CAPM, market efficiency. Includes Bloomberg Market Concepts.
investor

Financial Accounting

15 credits
Double-entry, 3-statement modelling, ratio analysis. Work with real annual reports.
builder

Quantitative Methods 1

15 credits
Probability, distributions, regression, hypothesis testing with R and Excel.
innovator

Corporate Finance

20 credits
WACC, DCF valuation, capital structure, dividend policy. Real-world case studies.
investor

Equity & Fixed Income Analysis

20 credits
DCF, comps, bond pricing, duration, credit analysis. Write an equity research report.
investor

Financial Modelling (Excel & Python)

15 credits
Build integrated 3-statement models, DCF with scenarios, and use Python/pandas.
builder

Derivatives & Risk Management

15 credits
Options (Black-Scholes), futures, swaps, Greeks, hedging strategies.
investor

Financial Econometrics

15 credits
Time series, volatility modelling, factor models. Apply to real market data.
innovator

Advanced Portfolio Management

20 credits
Mean-variance optimisation, factor investing, performance attribution.
investor

Alternative Investments

15 credits
Private equity, hedge funds, real assets. Build an LBO model.
builder

Behavioural Finance & ESG

15 credits
Cognitive biases, sustainable investing, impact measurement.
innovator

Applied Investment Project

30 credits
Capstone: manage a £100k virtual portfolio, pitch to industry panel.
builder · investor

+ electives: FinTech, Private Equity, Real Estate, International Finance, Mergers & Acquisitions
