📚 The Nolan MBA · Programme Structure

A carefully scaffolded journey from foundational tools to venture launch. Each term builds on the last, balancing depth, breadth, and real-world application.

🔨 TERM 1 · TOOLKIT
⚙️ TERM 2 · DEEP DIVES
🚀 TERM 3 · LAUNCH
TERM 1 45 CREDITS · 4 MODULES 🔨 THE BUILDER'S TOOLKIT

Foundational tools every entrepreneur, investor, and innovator must master. You'll build the core skills in finance, data, marketing, and mindset.

New Venture Finance 15 credits
Master cap tables, term sheets, valuation, convertible notes, and fundraising mechanics. Build dynamic financial models.
INVESTOR BUILDER
Entrepreneurial Mindset & Creativity 10 credits
Opportunity recognition, effectuation, customer discovery, and creative problem-solving. Conduct 20+ customer interviews.
BUILDER INNOVATOR
Data Analytics for Startups 10 credits
Python, pandas, cohort analysis, LTV/CAC, and data-driven decision making. Work with real startup datasets.
INNOVATOR INVESTOR
Marketing for Startups 10 credits
Growth marketing, acquisition channels, conversion optimisation, and brand building on a bootstrap budget.
BUILDER
📊 Typical weekly workload: 10-12 hours (New Venture Finance) + 8-10 hours each for other modules = ~40 hours total. Balanced for deep learning.
TERM 2 45 CREDITS · 4 MODULES ⚙️ THE INVESTOR'S DEEP DIVES

Specialised knowledge to evaluate, fund, and scale ventures. You'll move from toolkit to deep expertise in VC, product, and strategy.

Venture Capital & Private Equity 15 credits
LBO modelling, fund economics, carry waterfalls, deal sourcing, and due diligence. Build a full fund model.
INVESTOR BUILDER
Design Thinking & Product Development 10 credits
Prototyping (Figma), user testing, MVP definition, and iterative development. Build and test a product concept.
INNOVATOR BUILDER
Technology & Innovation Strategy 10 credits
Disruptive technology, IP strategy, platform ecosystems, and tech scouting. Prepare a tech scouting report.
INNOVATOR INVESTOR
Entrepreneurial Operations 10 credits
Scaling operations, supply chain, hiring, culture, and operational excellence for high-growth ventures.
BUILDER
📊 Typical weekly workload: 12-14 hours (VC/PE) + 8 hours each for other modules = ~40 hours. Deep dives require intense focus.
TERM 3 30 CREDITS · 3 MODULES 🚀 THE INNOVATOR'S LAUNCHPAD

Apply everything you've learned. Launch a real venture, lead corporate innovation, and engage with global ecosystems.

New Venture Creation Lab (Capstone) 15 credits
Launch a real venture or join a startup. Full 3-statement projections, unit economics, investor pitch deck, and Demo Day presentation.
BUILDER INVESTOR INNOVATOR
Corporate Innovation & Intrapreneurship 10 credits
Innovation accounting, internal venture structures, and pitching to the C-suite. Simulate a corporate innovation pitch.
INNOVATOR BUILDER
Global Immersion (Elective) 5 credits
One-week immersion in Silicon Valley or Singapore. Meet founders, VCs, and innovation leaders. Ecosystem mapping.
INNOVATOR INVESTOR
📊 Typical weekly workload: Venture Lab dominates (15-20 hours), plus 8-10 hours for other modules. Capstone intensity prepares you for real-world launch.

🧱 Why This Structure Works

Term 1 · Toolkit

You learn the language and tools of entrepreneurship. Every module builds a core competency you'll use in every subsequent course.

Term 2 · Deep Dives

You apply your toolkit to specialised domains. You learn to think like an investor, a product leader, and a strategist.

Term 3 · Launch

You synthesise everything. The Venture Lab is where you prove you can build, fund, and scale.
